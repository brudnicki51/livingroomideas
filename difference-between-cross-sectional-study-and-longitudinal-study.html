Difference between cross sectional study and longitudinal study

.

Difference between cross sectional study and longitudinal study. In a cross sectional study you collect data from a population at a specific point in time. Longitudinal studies span multiple sessions and the variables could change. The key difference between the two studies stem from the fact that while a cross sectional study presents the researcher with a cross sectional analysis of the research a longitudinal study presents a series of analysis in each phase of the research.
